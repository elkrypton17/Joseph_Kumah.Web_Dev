About Hon. Joseph Kwame Kumah

Biography

Joseph Kwame Kumah was born on January 24, 1974, in Sugliboi in the Bono East Region of Ghana. He is a dedicated politician, educator, and public servant who currently serves as the Member of Parliament for Kintampo North constituency.

As a proud member of the National Democratic Congress (NDC), he was elected to Parliament in the 2020 Ghanaian general election, winning with 33,460 votes (62.98% of the votes cast), demonstrating the strong support for NDC's vision of social democracy and inclusive development.

"The NDC stands for social democracy, equality, and inclusive development for all Ghanaians. As an NDC Member of Parliament, I am committed to these principles in serving my constituents."

Hon. Kumah is a member of the Judiciary Committee and also serves as the Vice Chairman of the Parliamentary Select Committee on Education, where he advocates for long-term planning and infrastructure development in Ghana's education sector.

Prior to his political career, Hon. Kumah served as a house master at the Ghana Education Service, bringing his educational expertise to his role as a public servant.

He is a Christian and continues to serve his constituents with dedication and commitment.

Achievements & Initiatives

Education Advocacy

As Vice Chairman of the Parliamentary Select Committee on Education, Hon. Kumah has been a strong advocate for long-term planning in Ghana's education sector, proposing a 75-year education plan to ensure sustainable development.

Infrastructure Development

Championed the need for increased investment in school infrastructure to successfully transition from the double-track system to single-track in senior high schools across Ghana.

Community Engagement

Actively engages with constituents through regular community visits, town hall meetings, and development initiatives in Kintampo North.

Legislative Work

Serves on the Judiciary Committee, contributing to legislative processes and governance in Ghana's Parliament.

Kintampo North Initiatives

Educational Transformation in Kintampo North

2021-Present Across Kintampo North

Hon. Joseph Kwame Kumah has spearheaded several educational initiatives in Kintampo North, focusing on improving access to quality education for all residents.

Key Achievements:

  • School Infrastructure: Facilitated the construction of 3 new classroom blocks at Kintampo Methodist Primary, Babatokuma D/A Primary, and Jema Senior High School.
  • Scholarship Program: Established the "Kintampo North Educational Fund" which has provided scholarships to over 200 underprivileged students to pursue secondary and tertiary education.
  • ICT Centers: Secured funding for the establishment of 2 ICT centers in Kintampo and Jema to enhance digital literacy among students.
  • Teacher Support: Implemented a teacher motivation program that provides additional incentives for teachers who serve in rural communities within the constituency.
  • Educational Materials: Distributed over 5,000 textbooks, exercise books, and other learning materials to schools across the constituency.

"Education is the most powerful tool we can use to transform Kintampo North. My commitment is to ensure that every child in this constituency has access to quality education regardless of their background."

- Hon. Joseph Kwame Kumah

Healthcare Improvement Projects

2020-Present Kintampo North Municipal

Hon. Kumah has prioritized healthcare access and quality in Kintampo North, working to improve facilities and services for all constituents.

Key Achievements:

  • Community Health Centers: Facilitated the renovation and equipment of 4 Community-Based Health Planning and Services (CHPS) compounds in rural areas of Kintampo North.
  • Medical Outreach: Organized bi-annual medical outreach programs that have provided free healthcare services to over 5,000 residents.
  • Maternal Health: Championed a maternal health initiative that has reduced maternal mortality by providing birthing kits and training for traditional birth attendants.
  • COVID-19 Response: Led the constituency's COVID-19 response team, securing PPE and medical supplies for healthcare facilities during the pandemic.
  • Ambulance Service: Successfully advocated for the allocation of two new ambulances to improve emergency medical services in the constituency.

"Healthcare is a fundamental right, not a privilege. I am committed to ensuring that every resident of Kintampo North has access to quality healthcare services regardless of their location or economic status."

- Hon. Joseph Kwame Kumah

Infrastructure Development Projects

2020-Present Various Locations

Infrastructure development has been a cornerstone of Hon. Kumah's work in Kintampo North, focusing on improving roads, water access, and public facilities.

Key Achievements:

  • Road Network: Secured funding for the rehabilitation of over 45km of feeder roads connecting farming communities to market centers.
  • Water Projects: Facilitated the drilling of 15 boreholes across various communities, providing clean water to over 10,000 residents.
  • Electrification: Extended electricity to 8 previously unconnected communities through the rural electrification program.
  • Community Centers: Constructed 3 multi-purpose community centers in Kintampo, Jema, and Babatokuma to serve as venues for community gatherings and events.
  • Market Facilities: Upgraded the Kintampo Central Market with new stalls, storage facilities, and sanitation infrastructure.

"Infrastructure is the backbone of development. By improving our roads, water systems, and public facilities, we are laying the foundation for economic growth and improved quality of life in Kintampo North."

- Hon. Joseph Kwame Kumah

Agricultural Development Programs

2021-Present Farming Communities

Agriculture is the main livelihood for many residents of Kintampo North. Hon. Kumah has implemented several initiatives to support farmers and enhance agricultural productivity.

Key Achievements:

  • Farmer Support: Distributed improved seeds, fertilizers, and farming equipment to over 2,000 smallholder farmers.
  • Irrigation Systems: Facilitated the development of small-scale irrigation systems in 5 farming communities to support year-round farming.
  • Cashew Development: Launched the Kintampo Cashew Development Initiative, providing 50,000 cashew seedlings to farmers to diversify their crops and increase income.
  • Agricultural Extension: Strengthened agricultural extension services by supporting the training and deployment of 15 additional extension officers.
  • Market Access: Established farmer cooperatives and linked them with buyers to improve market access and fair pricing for agricultural products.

"Agriculture is not just an occupation in Kintampo North; it's our way of life. By supporting our farmers with modern techniques and resources, we are ensuring food security and economic prosperity for our communities."

- Hon. Joseph Kwame Kumah

Youth Empowerment and Sports Development

2022-Present Across Kintampo North

Hon. Kumah has placed special emphasis on youth development, creating opportunities for skills acquisition, employment, and sports development.

Key Achievements:

  • Skills Training: Established the Kintampo Youth Skills Development Center, which has trained over 300 young people in various vocational skills.
  • Entrepreneurship Support: Created a youth entrepreneurship fund that has provided startup capital to 50 young entrepreneurs in the constituency.
  • Sports Infrastructure: Renovated the Kintampo Community Sports Field and provided sports equipment to 10 schools in the constituency.
  • Annual Sports Festival: Initiated the annual Kintampo North Inter-Community Sports Festival to promote unity and discover sporting talents.
  • Internship Program: Developed an internship program that places young graduates in various organizations to gain work experience.

"The youth are not just the future of Kintampo North; they are its present. By investing in their education, skills, and talents, we are building a stronger and more prosperous constituency."

- Hon. Joseph Kwame Kumah
